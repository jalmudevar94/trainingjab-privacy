Privacy Policy for TrainingJAB

Last updated: [Date]

Welcome to TrainingJAB. Your privacy is important to us. It is TrainingJAB's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our app.

This app is designed to work offline. All data you generate is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

1. Information We Store

    a. Information You Provide:
    - Settings: We store your app preferences, such as your chosen weight unit (kg/lb) and enabled equipment.
    - Favorites: We store the list of workouts you mark as favorites.
    - Contact: If you contact us via email, we will have your email address and the content of your message.

    b. Information We Store Automatically:
    - Workout History: We store a local record of your completed workouts, including the type, duration, rounds, reps, and other performance metrics.
    - Saved Session to Restore: We may temporarily store your progress in an ongoing workout so you can resume it later. This data is deleted once the session is completed or abandoned.

2. How We Use Your Information

    Your information is used exclusively to provide and improve the app's functionality:
    - To display your workout history and track your performance over time.
    - To customize your experience based on your settings.
    - To allow you to resume interrupted workouts.
    - To respond to your support requests.

3. Data Security

    All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal data on any external servers. The security of your data depends on the security of your device.

4. Data Sharing

    We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information with any third parties. All your data remains on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

    TrainingJAB is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.

7. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through the "Contact Us" section in the app's settings.

Data Deletion

All user-generated data, including workout history, custom workouts, and the list of favorites, is stored exclusively locally on the user's device. This data is not sent to any external server.

The complete and permanent deletion of all user data is accomplished by uninstalling the application from the device.
